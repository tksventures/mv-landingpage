For Token Buyers

The Tokes (TKS) token sale, or Initial Exchange Offering (IEO), is happening January 2020. Tokes is the native digital currency unit of the Multichain Ventures ecosystem - including the Tokes Platform, Cubed, EDEN, and Reli. Tokes is not only the native transactional token of the Multichain Ventures ecosystem, but has a number of economic features built into the token protocol, including staking, burning, and discount models. Learn more about the TKS token in our supporting documentation

Tokes (TKS) are not only intended for transactional purposes at the point of sale and for ecommerce purchases - with payments that settle in seconds. Holders of the token receive periodic allocations for staking (proof of stake: POS) Tokes in their mobile or desktop wallet. For services like supply chain data entires via EDEN and for subscription based fees of Tokes Platform, Cubed, or Reli services, Tokes can be burned to reduce recurring costs. Also, purchasing any or product service from Multichain Ventures using Tokes reduces the base cost of services. Learn more in our Tokenomics documentation.

What is Cubed?

Cubed is an off-the-shelf payment solution for merchants looking to offer their customers a crypto payments option. White label solutions are also available.

Cubed builds payment processing software for cryptocurrency adoptions in both physical brick and mortar businesses and for ecommerce. This includes point of sale software, web checkout components, payment APIs, consumer wallets, and custom block explorers, with integrations to popular inventory management and CRM software.

Learn More

What is the Tokes Platform?

Tokes (TKS) is the crypto token that powers the Multichain Ventures ecosystem. It is an end-to-end merchant gateway platform for companies looking to offer their customers a crypto-payment option.

Tokes Platform leverages blockchain technology to overcome hurdles within the legal cannabis industry. Federal prohibition has resulted in a lack of banking options for licensed cannabis businesses. By leveraging decentralized blockchain technology, Tokes provides financial freedom to these entities and enhances supply chain visibility of cannabis goods for business stakeholders, regulators, and consumers.

Learn More

What is EDEN?

Eden is a supply chain visibility toolset for the legal cannabis industry's unbanked business.

Multichain Ventures is developing the EPCIS compliant EDEN Blockchain to provide an immutable, decentralized ledger for supply chain visibility. While this technology serves the cannabis vertical via the Tokes Platform, the EDEN blockchain is fundamentally industry agnostic and can serve to ensure the providence of goods ranging from agriculture to technology to high end consumer goods.

Learn More

What is Reli.cloud?

Reli is a multi-cloud DevOps platform that provides development teams with open-source tools to automate and scale their blockchain infrastructure.

Reli.cloud combines cutting edge open-source technologies into an accessible Platform as a Service, automating DevOps needs and application publishing with high availability. Leveraging Kubernetes, Reli is multi-cloud, running on infrastructure including Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud Platform, and more. Reli enables teams to quickly deploy a number of software products, including blockchain infrastructure, with easy scalability.
